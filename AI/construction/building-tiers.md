# Building Tiers — Upgrade Costs and HP

Per building block (wall/foundation/floor are the same cost):

| Tier | Upgrade cost | HP | Notes |
|------|--------------|-----|-------|
| Twig | 50 wood (placement) | 10 | Burns, breaks with anything |
| Wood | 200 wood | 250 | Burns (fire arrows, molotov) |
| Stone | 300 stone | 500 | Soft side picked for free |
| Sheet Metal | 200 metal frags | 1000 | No soft side weakness to melee |
| Armored | 25 HQM | 2000 | Most expensive per HP |

Soft side vs hard side:
- Stone walls: soft (smooth) side takes full melee pickaxe damage — always
  place soft side INWARD. ~7 metal pickaxes break a stone wall for free.
- Sheet metal and armored have no melee-exploitable soft side.

Upkeep (per block per 24h, scales with base size):
- First bracket: 10% of build cost, grows to 33% for huge bases.
- Tool cupboard range covers building blocks only; deployables don't decay
  indoors with TC coverage.

Door HP quick reference:
- Wooden Door: 200 HP
- Sheet Metal Door: 250 HP
- Garage Door: 600 HP (no weak side, cheap per HP — the default choice)
- Armored Door: 800 HP

Honeycombing: layer cheap stone walls around the core so raiders pay sulfur
for every layer; triangle honeycomb is denser than squares.
