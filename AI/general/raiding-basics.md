# Raiding Basics — Eco Raid Costs

Sulfur is the real currency of raiding. Approximate sulfur cost to CRAFT each tool:

- Timed Explosive Charge (C4): 2200 sulfur each
- Rocket: 1400 sulfur each
- High Velocity Rocket: 200 sulfur each (low damage to buildings)
- Satchel Charge: 480 sulfur each
- Explosive 5.56 Rifle Ammo: 25 sulfur per round
- Beancan Grenade: 120 sulfur each
- F1 Grenade: 60 sulfur each
- Molotov: low sulfur, only good vs wooden structures

Common eco raid picks (cheapest sulfur for the job):

- Wooden door (200 HP): 1 satchel (480 sulfur) or ~18 explosive ammo (~450), or fire/melee for free
- Sheet metal door (250 HP): 1 C4 over-kill; cheapest is ~63 explosive ammo (~1575) or 4 satchels (1920); 2 beancan + ... avoid
- Garage door (600 HP): 9 satchels (4320) vs ~150 explosive ammo (~3750) vs 2 C4 (4400) — explosive ammo usually cheapest
- Armored door (800 HP): 2 C4 (4400) or ~200 explosive ammo (~5000)
- Stone wall (500 HP): 2 C4 (4400), 4 rockets (5600), 10 satchels (4800), ~185 explosive ammo (~4625) — soft side with pickaxes is FREE but slow and loud
- Sheet metal wall (1000 HP): 4 C4 / 8 rockets — expensive; go through doors instead
- Armored wall (2000 HP): 8 C4 — almost never worth it

Rules of thumb:
- Doors are almost always cheaper than walls. Raid through doors/windows first.
- Soft-side stone (the smooth side) can be picked with melee tools for free.
- Mixing one rocket + explosive ammo "finishing" often beats whole rockets.
- Ladders/twig towers bypass walls entirely — costs nearly nothing.
- Exact destroy tables per target come from live game data; trust those numbers
  over this file when they disagree.
